AI Attention Analysis Tool

As we know that in some scenarios in classroom,there may be some situations where the students may not be interested in a lecture,in such situations the learning outcome is not achieved.In order to overcome this problem, We have proposed a solution where the teaching effectiveness can be analyzed and if the attention of the students falls below a particular threshold,the teacher is notified about that so that the teacher may be more interactive . The project aims to analyze the attention of the students present in a classroom. The system will take the input in the form of video streams from a camera and with the help of Computer Vision, attention of students in the classroom is analyzed. The video stream is processed and the faces of the students present in the class are detected.After the detection of faces of students present,the attention of the students is analyzed. The data of the detected face is used and an algorithm is used to analyze the Attention of students. The Attention Index is calculated for the students in terms of group dynamics. After analyzing the attention , a Heat Map is generated for the classroom. The variation of Attention is shown with the help of the Heat Map generated for the classroom. If the Attention falls below a particular threshold , a notification is sent to the teacher.
